Humble Beginnings

In 2014, we set out to create a sports & fitness brand that would surpass all others. We aimed to create a "one-stop-shop" for all things fitness, health, and lifestyle. This meant creating an engaging community platform, quality nutritional supplement, unique apparel, and fitness advice backed by scientific data.

Our quest started when our team began to search for the best place to find fitness advice, supplements, and branding. Not because we wanted to compete, but because we were actually looking for fitness advice! Here at Vault Sports, all of our team members are active members of the fitness community, so we came about by creating a solution to a problem. When we couldn't find an effective solution, we decided to create one.

Today, our vision continues to stay steady, create the best sports and fitness brand that provides athletes of all types a home. At our foundation are a strong line of products that you can trust, and great people.

Our Guarantee & Values

We have a lifetime commmitment to focus our entire attention on each of our products to make them the highest quality possible. When we release a new line of products, you hear the story behind them, and the entire production process. In fact, we're so confident in our products that if you're not happy with your purchase, you can return it during the first 60 days, no questions asked.

Vault Sports is involved in the community, and we volunteer wherever we can. We see the value in giving back, as our brand would not be possible without our customers. Catch us at some of the most popular sporting events and conventions for samples, raffles, games and more.
